Putrajaya Sports, Tourism Expo & Forum 2025

In conjunction with the race pack collection for TWINCITY® MARATHON 2025

The Putrajaya Sports Tourism Expo will be hosting the Twincity Marathon for the first time ever and will features over 80 exhibitors offering the latest in running footwear, apparel, nutrition and technology. Additionally, the Expo is the home of the participant race pack collection for all Twincity Marathon participants. Held at Auditorium Tun Azizan Zainul Abidin, the three-day Expo is free and open to the public.

Looking forward to the participation from the various genres of the enterprises and groups of that can entertain each visitor regardless of age, gender, nationality, etc.
